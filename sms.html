SMS Program

SMS program details

Last updated: May 26, 2026 · Operated by Kaico LLC, Tulsa, Oklahoma

Plain English: If you call a business that uses Kaico Voice and give our AI receptionist your phone number for a booking, you may receive a text message confirming the appointment. You can reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages. This page explains exactly how the program works.

Brand and program

Brand Kaico LLC, doing business as Kaico Voice
Program name Kaico Voice Booking Confirmations
Use case Transactional account notifications (appointment confirmations and reminders) for customers of small service businesses that use Kaico Voice as their AI phone receptionist.
Message frequency Typically 1 to 3 messages per booking. Variable depending on whether you confirm, reschedule, or cancel.
Cost Message and data rates may apply, depending on your wireless carrier and plan. Kaico Voice does not charge you for SMS messages.
Supported carriers All major US carriers (AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, US Cellular) plus most regional carriers. T-Mobile is not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

How you opt in

Consent is captured verbally during your phone call with our AI receptionist. There is no online form or web checkbox. The exact flow is:

  1. You call a business that uses Kaico Voice (for example, a window tint shop or a hauling company).
  2. Our AI receptionist answers and helps you book or get information.
  3. When you confirm a booking, the receptionist tells you that a confirmation text will be sent to the number you provided, and that you may reply STOP at any time to opt out.
  4. Continuing the call and confirming the booking after that disclosure constitutes your opt-in.

Example of the verbal opt-in (sample transcript)

Receptionist (AI)

Got it. Confirming three loads of base rock to 5301 South Yale on Tuesday morning at 8 AM. Best callback number?

Caller

It's 918-555-0123.

Receptionist (AI)

Perfect. I'll text a confirmation to 918-555-0123 in a minute. You can reply STOP anytime to opt out, or HELP for help. You're all set for Tuesday.

This is the disclosure moment. Continuing past this point is the opt-in.

Caller

Sounds good, thanks.

How you opt out

  • Reply STOP to any message you receive from us. You will get a single confirmation that you have been unsubscribed, and you will not receive any further messages from that program.
  • Reply HELP for support information, or email trey@kaicovoice.com.

We never send marketing messages, promotional offers, or third-party advertising through this program. SMS is used only to confirm and coordinate the booking you made.

Privacy

Your phone number and any related booking information are handled according to our Privacy Policy. We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing.
